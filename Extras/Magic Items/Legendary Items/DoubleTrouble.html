Double Trouble

Magic Staff, Legendary, requires attunement by a Spellcaster

Lightning Power. While attuned to this weapon, you have resistance to lightning damage and a +2 to spell attack rolls and spell save DC. Additionally, this staff can be used to cast Lightning Bolt (5th Level) three times per day, and double cast for three charges.

Double Trouble. This staff has 10 charges. When casting a spell, you may use a number of charges equal to the spell’s level to cast that spell twice. Creatures make separate saves for both spells if they are in both, or you make separate attack rolls against the same or different targets, and you roll separate damage for each cast. If you cast two concentration spells, you are considered concentrating on one spell, making one save if you take damage, both ending when you end concentration.

Double Trouble regains all expended charges daily at dawn.
