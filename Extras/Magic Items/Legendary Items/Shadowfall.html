Shadowfall

Magic Weapon (Spear/Axe), Legendary, requires attunement

Magic Weapon. All attacks made with Shadowfall are magical. Shadowfall has a +3 to all attack and damage rolls made with it.

Mult-Functional. Shadowfall is a custom-built spear with axe blades with the finesse property and three methods of attacking.

Spear Stab: 1d10 piercing damage.

Axe Slash: 1d12 slashing damage (two-handed). Shadowfall’s axe blades are retractable.

Spear Throw: 2d10 piercing damage. (Range 60/120 ft)

Returning. Shadowfall returns to the user when thrown. However, a user could choose to pull themselves to Shadowfall instead, provided it is lodged in something.

Shadowfall was custom-built by Maron Shadowbreaker. He got it upgraded at Scara El Sharahh.
