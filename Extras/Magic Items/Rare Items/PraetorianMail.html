Praetorian Mail

Magic Armor (Breast Plate), Rare

Tougher. This set of breast plate provides 16 AC + dex (maximum of 2). You have disadvantage on stealth checks.

Acid Protection. While wearing this armor, you have resistance to acid damage.

Not to be confused with the Praetorian Mail armor from the Upside, this armor is a magical variant of that version.
