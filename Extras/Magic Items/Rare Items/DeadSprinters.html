Dead Sprinters

Wondrous Item (Boots), rare, requires attunement

Dead Sprint. Your move speed increases by 5 feet. On your turn, when you run out of movement, you can take 1d6 damage (which cannot be reduced in any way) to increase your move speed by 5 feet. You can do this a number of times equal to your level per turn. This also cannot reduce you below zero hit points.
