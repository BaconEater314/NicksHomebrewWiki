Stars of Ruin

6th Level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 90ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a large barrage of 12 glintstone stars at enemies within range. You can choose to fire up to 4 at one creature in range, making a ranged spell attack roll for each, dealing 2d8 force damage per hit.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, you conjure an additional star and increase the amount of stars that can target one person by 1 for each spell slot above 6th.

Spell Lists: Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
