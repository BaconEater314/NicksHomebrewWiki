Rykard’s Rancor

6th Level Magma Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a large flaming skull that travels across the battlefield, leaving explosions in its wake. This skull has a movement of 60 feet, can move through other creatures’ spaces, and cannot move diagonally. Every creature that this skull passes through must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 10d6 fire damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. A creature only makes this saving throw once per casting of this spell.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 and the move speed by 10 for each slot level above 6th.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
