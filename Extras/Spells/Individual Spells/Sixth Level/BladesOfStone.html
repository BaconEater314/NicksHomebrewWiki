Blades of Stone

6th Level Gravity Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 90ft

Components: Instantaneous

Duration: V, S

You call forth the earth, bending it to you will around a point within range. All creatures in a 30ft radius sphere must make a Strength saving throw, taking 4d8 piercing damage and 4d8 bludgeoning damage and being knocked prone on a failure or half as much on a success with no adverse effects.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for both damage types for each slot level above 6th.

Spell Lists: Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
