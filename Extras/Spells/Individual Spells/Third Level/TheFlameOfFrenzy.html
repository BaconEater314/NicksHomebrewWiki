The Flame of Frenzy

3rd Level Frenzied Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (20ft Cone)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You release a burst of yellow flame tendrils from your eyes in a 20ft cone. Each target in the area must make an Intelligence saving throw or take 4d8 fire damage, with half as much on a success. Creatures who take damage gain a Madness buildup equal to 1/3 of the damage they take. Additionally, the caster gains 1d10 madness buildup.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 3rd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Paladin
