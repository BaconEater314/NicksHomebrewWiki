Greatblade Phanlanx

3rd Level Carian Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute

You conjure a small group of 3 large glinstone blades that float about your head, which last for 1 minute. Whenever a creature of your choice walks within 20 feet of you, all 3 blades fire at it. Target must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 10d6 force damage on a failure, or half as much on a success.

Creatures have disadvantage on this saving throw if it triggers immediately after you cast this spell.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you conjure an additional blade (dealing an extra 2d6) for each spell slot above 3rd.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
