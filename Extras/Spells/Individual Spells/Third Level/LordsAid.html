Lord’s Aid

3rd Level Two Fingers Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (20ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You bless all creatures of your choice within 20 feet (including yourself), removing any blood loss, scarlet rot buildup, and curing poison and scarlet rot. Creatures asleep due to magical means are also awakened.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the radius increases by 5 feet for each slot level above 3rd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Paladin
