Ancient Dragon’s Lightning Strike

3rd Level Dragon Cult Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (20ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Drawing on ancient draconic lightning, you cause red lightning to strike around you. All creatures within 20 feet of you must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 6d6 AD Lightning damage on a fail or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid
