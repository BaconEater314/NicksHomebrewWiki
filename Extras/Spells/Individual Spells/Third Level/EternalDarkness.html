Eternal Darkness

3rd Level Sellian Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 30ft

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentrations)

You create a small sphere of pure darkness at a point within range. All 3rd-level or lower spells cast within 30 feet of the sphere that fire projectiles are drawn to the sphere, either missing or detonating in that area. Line spells that move through the area are curved 45 degrees towards the sphere.

Spells that fire projectiles usually make ranged spell attack rolls or have it in their description, such as fireball.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, spells of equal level or lower are pulled into the sphere.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
