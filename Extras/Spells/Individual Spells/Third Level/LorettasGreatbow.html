Loretta’s Greatbow

3rd Level Carian Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 150ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a large glintstone bow and fire one arrow at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack roll, dealing 5d8 force damage on a hit, or half as much on a miss.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 2d8 for each slot level above 3rd as you knock another arrow.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
