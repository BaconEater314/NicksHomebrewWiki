Shooting Star

3rd Level Evocation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 90ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You hurl a streaking star from the palm of your hand into a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 8d8 force damage and is revealed until the end of their next turn. A revealed creature cannot benefit from the invisible condition.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 per slot above 3rd level.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
