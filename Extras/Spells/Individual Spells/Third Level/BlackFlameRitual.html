Black Flame Ritual

3rd Level Black Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (10ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Hand alight with black flames, you draw up black flames around you in a 10ft radius centered on you. All creatures in the area (not including you) must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 7d6 black fire damage on a fail or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 and the radius by 5 feet for each slot level above 3rd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid
