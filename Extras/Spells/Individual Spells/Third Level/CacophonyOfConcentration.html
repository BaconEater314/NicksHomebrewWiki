Cacophony of Concentration

3rd Level Enchantment

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V,

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You touch a willing creature within range. The target gains supernatural concentration on either physical or mental tasks.

Physical concentration gives advantage on Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution rolls.

Mental concentration gives advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma rolls.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the target gains a +1 bonus to their rolls, +2 at 6th, and +3 at 8th.

Spell Lists: Bard
