Crystal Barrage

Glintstone Sorcery Cantrip

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (15ft Line)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a small barrage of small glintstone shards in a 15ft long, 5 ft wide line, striking the first enemy it comes in contact with. Affected creatures must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d6 force damage on a fail. Creatures within 5 feet of you when you cast this spell have disadvantage on the saving throw.

At Higher Levels: This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (3d6), 11th level (4d6) and 17th level (5d6).

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
