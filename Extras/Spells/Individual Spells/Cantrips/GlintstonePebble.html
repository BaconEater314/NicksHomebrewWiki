Glintstone Pebble

Glintstone Sorcery Cantrip

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 90ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a small blue shard and fire it at an enemy within range. Make a ranged spell attack roll, dealing 1d6 + your spellcasting modifier force damage on a hit.

At Higher Levels: This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6) and 17th level (4d6).

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
