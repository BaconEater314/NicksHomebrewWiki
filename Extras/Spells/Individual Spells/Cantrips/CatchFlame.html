Catch Flame

Giant’s Flame Incantation Cantrip

Casting Time: Bonus Action

Range: Self

Components: S

Duration: Instantaneous

You quickly create a momentary spark in your hand to burn a nearby enemy. Make a melee spell attack roll, dealing 1d4 fire damage on a hit.

At Higher Levels. This spell’s damage increases by 1d4 when you reach 5th level (2d4), 11th level (3d4), and 17th level (4d4).

Spell Lists: Artificer, Cleric, Druid
