Lightning Spear

1st Level Dragon Cult Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 120 Feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a bolt of lightning and hurl it at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack roll, dealing 3d8 lightning damage on a hit. When you hit a creature wearing metal armor with this spell, you deal extra lightning damage equal to your spellcasting ability modifier.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 per spell slot above 1st level.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
