Carian Slicer

First Level Carian Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self

Components: S

Duration: Instantaneous

You summon a blade at the end of your staff that slices through objects and foes alike. Make a melee spell attack roll, dealing 1d8 + your spellcasting modifier Force damage on a hit. If you benefit from extra attack or have another action on your turn, you may swing the magic blade again on your turn without expending a spell slot.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
