Great Glintstone Shard

1st Level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 90 Feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a large glintstone shard and fire it at an enemy within range. Make a ranged spell attack roll, dealing 2d8 + your spell attack modifier force damage on a hit.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
