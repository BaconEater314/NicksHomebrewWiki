Shard Spiral

2nd Level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You summon a dual spiral of Glinstone magic that flies at an enemy within range. Make two ranged spell attack rolls against the target, dealing 2d8 force damage per hit.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 per hit for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
