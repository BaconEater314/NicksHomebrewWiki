Blood Flame Talons

2nd Level Blood Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (15ft Cube)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You slash through the air, igniting the air before you in bloody flames. Each creature in a 15-foot cube originating from you must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 fire damage on a fail or half as much on a success. A creature gains Bloodloss buildup equal to half the damage taken.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
