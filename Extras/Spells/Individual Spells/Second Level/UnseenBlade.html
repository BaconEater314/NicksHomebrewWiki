Unseen Blade

2nd Level Sellian Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S, M (a weapon wielded by a creature)

Duration: 1 hour (Concentration)

You touch a weapon, imbuing it with magical cloaking, turning it invisible for the duration. This effect ends if the weapon is used to deal damage. Attacks with this weapon while it is invisible are made with advantage.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional weapon within 5 feet of you per spell slot above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
