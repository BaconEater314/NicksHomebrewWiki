Frenzied Burst

2nd Level Frenzied Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 120ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You fill your eyes with the Frenzied Flames, unleashing a maddening bolt towards a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack roll, dealing 6d6 fire damage on a hit. A creature takes madness buildup equal to 1/3 the damage taken. Additionally, the caster gains 1d10 madness buildup.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.When you cast this spell using a spell slot of (level+1) level or higher, the damage increases by (damage die) per spell slot above (level) level.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Paladin
