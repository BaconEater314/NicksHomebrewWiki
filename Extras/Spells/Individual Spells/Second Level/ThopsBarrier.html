Thop’s Barrier

2nd Level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: Reaction

Range: Self

Components: S

Duration: Instantaneous

When targeted with a ranged spell attack, you may use your reaction to try to deflect it. This spell automatically deflects 2nd level and lower ranged spell attacks. For higher-level spells, make a check using your spellcasting ability, the DC being 10 + the spell's level. On a success, the spell is deflected elsewhere, and on a failure, the spell slot used to cast this spell slot is not wasted.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, it automatically deflects spells of equal level and lower.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
