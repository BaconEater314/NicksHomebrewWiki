Vyke’s Dragonbolt

2nd Level Dragon Cult Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 120ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a bolt of ancient dragon lightning and hurl it at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack roll, dealing 3d12 AD lightning damage on a hit. When you hit a creature wearing metal armor with this spell, you deal extra lightning damage equal to your spellcasting ability modifier.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d12 for each slot level above 1st.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
