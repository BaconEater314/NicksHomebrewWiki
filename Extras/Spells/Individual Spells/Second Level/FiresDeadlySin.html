Fire’s Deadly Sin

2nd Level Giant’s Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (10ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Hands alight with flames, you slam them into the ground, causing flames to burst around you in a 10ft radius centered on you. All creatures in the area must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 fire damage on a fail or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 and the radius by 5 feet for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid
