Beastial Claw

2nd Level Beastial Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (15ft Cone)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You drag your hand through the ground, ripping the earth in a great slashing motion in a 15ft cone. All creatures in the cone must make a Strength saving throw, taking 3d10 slashing damage on a fail and are knocked prone, or half as much on a success, evading prone.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid
