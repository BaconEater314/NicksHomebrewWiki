Bloodboon

2nd Level Blood Oath Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 30ft

Components: V, S, M (blood on the ground)

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You twirl your hand in the air, drawing nearby blood on the ground before tossing it aside, creating a line of boiling flames. You create a 5ft wide, 20ft long line at a point within range. All creatures that start their turn or enter the space for the first time must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 3d8 fire damage on fail, or half as much on success. Creatures gain bloodloss buildup equal to half the damage taken.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid
