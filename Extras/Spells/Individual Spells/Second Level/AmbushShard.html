Ambush Shard

2nd Level Sellian Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60ft

Components: S

Duration: Instantaneous

You summon a dark blade behind a creature within range. The target must make a Perception check contested with your Spell Save DC. On a failure, you gain advantage on the following attack. Make a ranged spell attack, dealing 4d6 force damage on a hit.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 and the DC increases by 2 for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
