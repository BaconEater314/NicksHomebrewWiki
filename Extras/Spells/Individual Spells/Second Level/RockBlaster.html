Rock Blaster

2nd Level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (10ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You charge up energy at the bottom of your staff before thrusting it into the ground. All creatures within 10 feet of you must make a Dexterity Saving throw, taking 3d8 force damage on a failure, or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
