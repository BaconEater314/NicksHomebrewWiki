Howl of Shibriri

2nd Level Frenzied Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (20ft Sphere)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You grasp your eyes, Frenzied Flames filling them as you let out a maddening howl. All creatures within 20 feet must make an Intelligence saving throw, taking 3d10 psychic damage, or half as much on a fail. A creature gains madness buildup equal to half the damage taken. Additionally, the caster gains madness buildup equal to half the damage rolled.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Cleric. Druid, Paladin
