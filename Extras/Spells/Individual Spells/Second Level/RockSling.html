Rock Sling

2nd Level Gravity Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 45ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You pull three rocks from the ground and fling them at a single enemy within range. Make three ranged attack rolls, dealing 2d6 bludgeoning damage per hit. If the terrain is not rocky enough to cast this spell, it fails and the spell slot is not wasted.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you summon an additional rock beyond the first 3 for each level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
