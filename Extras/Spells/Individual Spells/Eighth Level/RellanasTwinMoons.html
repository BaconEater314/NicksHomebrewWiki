Rellana's Twin Moons

8th Level Moon Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (20ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You levitate yourself into the air and curl into a ball, forming a large moon around you. Your moon creates a twin that slams into the ground shortly before yours does. All creatures in a 20ft radius sphere (not including you) must make 2 Dexterity saving throws, taking 8d6 force damage per failure, or half as much of each on a success.

A creature that fails the first of the two saving throws has disadvantage on the second.

You cannot cast this spell if your move speed is zero.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 9th level, the damage increases by 2d6 for both moons.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
