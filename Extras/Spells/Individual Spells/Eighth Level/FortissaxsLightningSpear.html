Fortissax's Lightning Spear

8th Level Dragon Cult Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (25ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Channeling the pinnacle of ancient dragon magic, you summon two massive spears and slam them into the ground. All creatures within 25 feet of you must make two dexterity saving throws, taking 8d6 AD Lightning damage per failure, or half as much of each on a success.

A creature that fails the first of the two saving throws has disadvantage on the second.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell at 9th level, the damage increases to 10d6 for both saves, and the radius increases to 30 feet.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid
