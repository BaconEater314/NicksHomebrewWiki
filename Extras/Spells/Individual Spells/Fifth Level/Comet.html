Comet

5th Level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 90ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a large glinstone comet at a creature within range. Make a spell attack roll, dealing 12d6 force damage on a hit.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by 2d6 for each slot level above 5th.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
