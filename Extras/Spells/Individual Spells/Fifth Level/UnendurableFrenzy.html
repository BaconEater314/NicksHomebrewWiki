Unendurable Frenzy

5th Level Frenzied Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (15ft Cone, 20ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

With frenzied flames in your eyes, you blast out flames in a 15-foot cone in front of you. Creatures in the area must make an Intelligence saving throw, taking 4d6 fire damage on a success, or half as much on a failure. A creature that takes damage gains madness buildup equal to 1/3 of the damage taken.

While concentrating, you can choose to recast this spell as an action on your turn without expending a spell slot. The caster gains 1d6 madness buildup every time they cast this spell.

Whenever you drop concentration, you release a burst of frenzied flames all around you. All creatures within 20 feet of you must make an Intelligence saving throw, taking 6d6 fire damage on a fail or half as much on a success. A creature that takes damage gains madness buildup equal to 1/3 of the damage taken, and the caster gains 1d6 madness buildup.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, all damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 5th.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Paladin
