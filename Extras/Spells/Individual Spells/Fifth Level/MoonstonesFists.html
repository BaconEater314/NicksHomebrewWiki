Moonstone's Fists

5th Level Conjuration

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You conjure a pair of gauntleted spectral fists over the target’s hands, who have fine control over them. While the spell lasts, the target can use an action to make two melee attacks with either the caster’s spellcasting ability or their strength, dealing 4d8 force damage on a hit. The caster can make these attacks as part of the casting of the spell.

If the target benefits from any sort of extra attack feature, they may make 1 additional attack per extra attack they have.

Doing enough damage on one attack with these fists may knock a creature prone based on its size. If the required damage is met, the creature must make a Strength Saving Throw against the spellcaster’s Spell Save DC or be knocked prone.

Creature Size Damage Required to Knock Prone
Tiny None
Small 10
Medium 15
Large 20
Huge 30
Gargantuan 40

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 5th

Spell Lists: Artificer, Cleric, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
