Scarlet Aeonia

7th Level Servants of Rot Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (30ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You rise up into the air, a scarlet aeonia forming around you. You come crashing down at a point within 30 feet, the aeonia blooming into a massive explosion. All creatures within 30 feet of where you land must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 8d10 Scarlet Rot damage on a fail or half as much on a success. A creature takes scarlet rot buildup equal to 1/3 the damage it takes.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th level or higher, the damage increases by 2d10 for each slot level above 7th.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid
