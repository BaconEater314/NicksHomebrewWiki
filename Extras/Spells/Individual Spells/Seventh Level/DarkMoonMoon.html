Ranni's Dark Moon/Rennalla's Full Moon

7th Level Moon Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 90ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You levitate yourself into the air and curl into a ball, forming a large moon around you. Ejecting itself out, it fires itself at a target point within range. All creatures within a 20ft radius sphere must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 12d6 force damage on failure, or half as much on a success.

You cannot cast this spell if your move speed is zero.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th level or higher, the damage increases by 2d6 for each slot level above 7th.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
