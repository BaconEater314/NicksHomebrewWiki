Black Blade

4th Level Erdtree Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure the Black Blade of death, swinging it at a creature within 10 feet. Make a melee spell attack roll, dealing 4d6 slashing damage and 4d6 black fire damage on a hit.

This blade also creates a wave of black flames in the direction you swing, hitting the first target beyond the melee target within 30ft. Make a ranged spell attack roll, ignoring disadvantage from being in enemy melee range, dealing 4d6 black fire damage on a hit.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, all black fire damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 4th.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Paladin
