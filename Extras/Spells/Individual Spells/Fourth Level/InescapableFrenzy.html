Inescapable Frenzy

4th Level Frenzied Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Eyes filled with frenzied flames, you grab a creature and blast frenzied flames into its face. Target a creature within 5 feet, forcing an Intelligence saving throw, taking 6d10 fire damage on a fail or half as much on a success. A creature gains madness buildup equal to 1/3 of the damage taken. Additionally, the caster gains 1d10 madness buildup.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 4th.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Paladin
