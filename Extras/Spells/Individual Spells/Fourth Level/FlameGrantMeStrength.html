Flame Grant Me Strength

4th Level Giant’s Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You touch a creature, blessing it with great fire powers. The target gains fire resistance, and whenever it deals fire damage, it may roll twice with each die, taking the higher result for each roll.

Spell Lists: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer
