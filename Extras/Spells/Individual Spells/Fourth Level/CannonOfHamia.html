Cannon of Hamia

4th level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 90ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You fire a large blast of magical energy at a target location within range. All creatures in a 20ft radius sphere must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 6d10 force damage or half as much on a failure.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a 5th spell slot or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 4th.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
